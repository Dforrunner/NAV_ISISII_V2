The ActiveCGM control will be downloaded and installed.
Remember to click "Yes" when asked if you want to install.
When a big line drawing / graphic appears in the big box below, it is complete.
While downloading, a small box with colored shapes should be in the upper left of the box.
If a "X" appears in the small box, then the download/install did not complete.
If this is the case, check your Internet Security options and make sure they are not set to High.
If the box is completely blank, you may have to restart your computer.
